My name is Rina Carmel, I'm a professional artist who specializes in oil color paintings. Been studying art in "Wiso Tasrfat School" in my youths.

The painting technique I have developed in recent years is characterized by light brush strokes.Over the years I have made many artworks. In addition, I can take to a journey to find your own special painting and create it for you.

 Let me bring the love of colors to you.
The gallery has opened in the home we have made for our family. It's located in a beautiful garden, peaceful and calm. The big range of paintings that are there includes many repertoires: landscape, models, nature, and nude art.

The gallery events taking place almost every month, please check our social media to find about the upcoming event. You are more than welcome to contact us for special visits.

Address: Hashmonaim 15, Hod Hasharon, Israel
If you ever had a dream to learn how to paint by yourself, you can get my guidance. There are weekly group classes in our gallery, you will get all the equipment required and get new skills. There are two group types, one is an adults group and the second group is for children.
